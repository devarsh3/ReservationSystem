Reservations or Takeouts can be chosen from the top right buttons

To Delete a Reservation/Takeout,
Type the Name of customer
Then Click "Delete"/"Paid"

To Clear reservations/takeouts,
Click on the "Clear Reservations/Takeout" Button

Reservations/Takeouts can be sorted by time

Username,Password, Menu, Timings, Help pages can be changed through "Privacy Settings"
